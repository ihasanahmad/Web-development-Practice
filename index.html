Zootopia 2 Trailer

Description

The trailer shows Judy Hopps and Nick Wilde working as partners. They are assigned a challenging new case involving snakes, a species that apparently hasn't been seen in Zootopia for a very long time. Their boss warns them that if they fail this case, he will split them up. Their investigation leads them to a neighborhood that was cut off from the rest of Zootopia when the city was founded. There, they discover a secret population of reptiles. It appears Judy and Nick are working to prove that the snakes are not the villains, as Judy states they need to help an "innocent snake".
